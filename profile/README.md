## Bookmarkd

**Bookmarkd** is a web platform designed for users to discover, track, review, and share their favorite books with other users. From avid readers searching for their next book to casual readers simply looking for their next weekend read, Bookmarkd offers both personalized recommendations powered by AI and a social ecosystem that allows users to form new connections and discover new books.
